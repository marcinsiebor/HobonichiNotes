About

Welcome to the Hobonichi Techo Planner! We aim to bring the essence of the popular Hobonichi Techo planner into the digital world.

Our website offers a Kanban Board for task management, a Note Taking application for jotting down ideas and thoughts, and more.

To add items, simply click the button and start writing. To delete, double tap the item and confirm whether or not you'd like to delete it

With the Hobonichi Techo website, you can stay organized, enhance your productivity, and bring structure to your daily life.
